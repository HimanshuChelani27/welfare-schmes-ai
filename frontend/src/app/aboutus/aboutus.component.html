About Our Project

Welfare AI is a dynamic digital platform designed to streamline the dissemination of information about government schemes in India. Utilizing advanced AI technology, Welfare AI offers personalized guidance to citizens seeking to understand and leverage various government initiatives. The platform features an intuitive interface, allowing users of all backgrounds to easily access scheme details such as eligibility, application processes, and benefits. With Welfare AI, essential government resources are just a few clicks away, empowering citizens and enhancing governmental transparency.
